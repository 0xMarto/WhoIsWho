Load your own set

Who is Who allows the player to upload his own set of cards.

The set must be uploaded as a compressed .zip file. This .zip file must have:

24 images (in a jpg or png format). The file name will be the name you choose for the face. Each image must show a character face. Also, the image has to have a width of 185 to 205 pixels and a height of 185 to 210 pixels.

One .txt file in which each face will be described. The description must follow some format too:

The .txt will have 24 lines, one for each face. In each line will appear the Name of the face, the hair color, the sex (M or F), and other true or false features.

Here it's showed the order of features and an example of how should be filled:

About & Rules

Who is who is an online two player guessing game.

How to play

Who is Who is a very simple game to play!

Each player is first randomly assigned a name from one of the cards on the board. By asking questions back and forth, you have to guess the other player's card.

The questions you are allowed to ask are predefined but are more than enough to figure out your opponent's card!

About cheating

Who is who allows cheating during the game, but with an interesting twist. Whenever you choose to cheat, your "lie counter" will increase by one.

When the game finished, the player with the most lies on their counter will LOSE!
